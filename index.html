About Me

I am an engineer by profession and a self-taught artist by passion. While engineering trained me to think structurally and analytically, painting allows me to explore emotion, intuition, and freedom.

As a self-learned artist, I have developed my style through consistent practice, experimentation, and curiosity rather than formal training. Every artwork I create reflects a balance between precision and imagination, structure and spontaneity.

For me, art is not just a hobby; it is a space where logic meets emotion and ideas turn into color.

Priyanka.
